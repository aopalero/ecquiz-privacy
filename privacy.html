Privacy Policy | Effective Date: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how eCQuiz ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your data. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students and teachers while ensuring high standards of academic integrity.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a secure and functional quiz experience, we collect:

  • Personal Identity: Your Name, Email address, and Student Number for authentication.
  • Academic Performance: Quiz responses, scores, and completion timestamps.
  • Proctoring Data: Limited violation logs (e.g., app switching or screenshot attempts) to maintain exam integrity.
  • Media Access: Camera access is used solely for QR code scanning and optional identity verification.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively for educational purposes:

  • To synchronize progress between student devices and teacher dashboards.
  • To generate analytics and scores for teacher review.
  • To prevent unauthorized use of the device during active quiz sessions via our Anti-Cheat technology.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes Supabase for secure backend data storage and authentication. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Rights & Security

All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols. Users have the right to access their data or request account deletion by contacting their school administrator or our support team.

6. Children's Privacy

As an educational tool, eCQuiz complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We only collect data necessary for school-authorized educational activities.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: support@aopalero.dev
