eCQuiz Data Management

Privacy Policy & Account Deletion Requests

1. Data Collection

eCQuiz collects your Name, Email, and Quiz Performance data to facilitate educational assessments.

🔴 Account & Data Deletion Request

Users of eCQuiz can request the permanent deletion of their account and all associated data by following these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@aopalero.dev
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Registered Email]"
  3. Our team will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days.

What is deleted: Your profile information, all created quizzes, and all stored student scores. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
